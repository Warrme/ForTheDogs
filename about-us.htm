About Us

For the Dogs is committed to helping our furry friends. We take in rescued dogs, strays, etc. and give them homes. Our mission is to ensure each and every dog we bring in is taken care of, loved, and trained to be good pet. We also sell food and toys to help you take care of your dog. We appreciate your time in visiting our website and hope you are willing to give a dog a new home!
